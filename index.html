ABOUT US

Express Wash dobby, our dobby offers a self-service laundry where the customer just needs to put their clothes in the machine and wait for it to finish washing and use the dryer to dry the clothes. Self-services laundry is very suitable for those people that are busy. As the laundry is a self-service laundry, it is operated for 24 hours. So, the customers are free to come to the dobby to do the laundry anytime. There are a lot of benefits of self-services laundry such as it can help to reduce the laundry cost, it is fast and convenient, affordable and can wash everything. Self-service laundry is the best option for everyone to wash their clothes.
